Meals at Numbers

At Numbers, our menu celebrates the diversity of Nigerian cuisine with an array of mouthwatering dishes to tantalize your taste buds. Indulge in classics like white rice with stew, jollof rice, and fried rice, or savor traditional favorites such as amala, eba, semo, and pounded yam served with soups like ewedu, gbegiri, egusi, and vegetable soup. For a fushion twist, explore our stir-fry spaghetti, where global flavors meet local flair, offering something to delight every palate.

Food at Numbers

Savour the culinary delight at Numbers, where each dish is a masterpiece crafted with passion and precision. From savoury freshly served meals to satisfying drinks, our menu offers a tantalizing array of flavors to satisfy every palate. Using only the freshest locally-sourced ingredients, our chefs skillfully blend traditional techniques with innovative twists, ensuring each bite is a symphony of taste and texture. Whether you crave comfort classics or adventurous creations, dining at Numbers promises an unforgettable gastronomic experience.
